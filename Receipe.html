How To Cook Carbonara Pasta 

Ingredients
  • 2 Table spoon of Cooking oil
  • 300g cooked fettecine or spagahetti Pasta
  • 1/4 Cup minced Garlic
  • 1/2 cup sliced button mushroom
  • 180g Canned tuna in oil
  • 1 pack of all purpose cream (250ml)
  • 1/2 Cup of evaporated milk
  • 1/2 Cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of permesan 
Here are the steps
  1. Heat cooking oil in pan
  2. Add garlic and cook until almost brown
  3. Add mushroom and tuna cook for another minute
  4. Pour in all purpose cream and evaporated milk
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Add water and milk to fully incorporate
  7. Toss in cooked pasta noodles into the sauce and combine until noodles are full coated with sauce
  8. Sprinkle grated cheese on top
  9. Serve Immediately
